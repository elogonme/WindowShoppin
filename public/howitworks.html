How It Works

You love checkin' out the goods in the windows of your favourite shopping district. Why not bring that experience online, and never forget what you've dreamed about buyin'? With Window Shoppin', you get that same experience, but way better!

Get Started!

After you create an account, open another window and go shoppin'! When you find an item you like, copy the url to the "add item" field on the WindowShoppin' page. Click add item, and voilà! It's saved to your own personal shopping cart, and you won't forget you wanted that goodie!

Save Dough!

You don't have to go lookin' to see if your goodies went on sale. Just go to Window Shoppin' ! Use the refresh feature and leave the work to us! In no time, you'll see how much you're savin'!

Get Organized!

Shoppin' for shoes, chairs or other wares? Add your personalized category to your items to keep them all together. Don't want to forget why you saved an obscure find? Write yourself a note to keep it top of mind!
